What is The Plan?

The Plan is a personal daily journal app that lives on your phone's home screen. Each day you check off your habits, log what you did across your focus areas, and write a quick note. Your entries sync to the cloud so they're available on any device.

Getting started
1 — Install on your phone
Open this app in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android). Tap the share icon and choose Add to Home Screen. The Plan will appear as a full-screen app on your home screen.
2 — Make it yours
Go to the Customize tab to set up your own daily checklist items and focus areas. Your customizations are saved to your code and sync across all your devices.
3 — Use it daily
Open The Plan each day, check off your habits, log your activities, and hit Save today. Your entry syncs to the cloud instantly.
The tabs
Today
Your daily checklist, focus areas, mood check-in, and notes. Hit Save today when done.
History
A calendar view of past entries. Tap any day to see what you logged.
Stats
Monthly breakdown of your streak, perfect days, habit completion rates, and mood patterns.
Customize
Add, rename, or remove checklist items and focus areas. Changes sync across your devices.
Settings
Toggle dark mode, change the accent color, and sign out of this device.
Focus area types
Log
Free-text entries — great for logging what you listened to, read, or watched.
Tags
Tap preset options to mark them done — ideal for exercise types or activities.
Checks
Checkboxes for a fixed list of items — good for recurring sub-habits.
Tasks
Add tasks on the fly each day and check them off — perfect for chores or to-dos that change daily.
Tips
· Save your entry before closing the app — it won't auto-save.
· Your data is tied to your access code. If you use the same code on multiple devices, they share the same journal.
· Clearing your browser data will sign you out, but your entries are safe in the cloud — just sign back in with your code.
· Dark mode preference is saved per device, not per code.
